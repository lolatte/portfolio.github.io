Study Buddy

Overview

Study Buddy is a mini project inspired by a random conversation with a friend about exam periods and the need to go to campus every night to meet with other students for night time group study.

Contributors

I worked on this alone.
PS: this is solely to show off my UI design skill.

As a mini project, I only designed a few pages for this. The general idea was to design a platform that enables students to meet up and study without having to commute or risk their safety. While ideating, I figured limiting the app's usage to group studies is a waste of potential. So, I went online to find out other things students would like then included related functionalities. E.g. Class reminders, performance statistics etc.

I also added streaks because studying gets overwhelming and having something as little as a fire symbol to cheer you on can be encouraging. Students can also be competitive, bragging about their streaks could be a source of pride.

Overall, I had so much fun designing this because it was challenging. For one, it's an unfamiliar territory as we do not have lots of apps out there for comparison. Picking the right colours to work with and arranging contents on a larger screen was something I also spent considerable time on but it all came together in the end.
